Our Story

Meet Our Founder

TO

Tochukwu Chidera Okeke

Founder & CEO, Praise Dynasty Real Estate Ltd

I was born and raised in Enugu State, Nigeria, where my early experiences shaped my values of discipline, growth, and creating meaningful impact. After completing my secondary education, I pursued higher studies at the University of Nigeria, where I graduated with a degree in Microbiology.

Along my journey, I explored different business opportunities across various industries, which strengthened my understanding of value creation, business development, and long-term vision. My transition into real estate was driven by a deeper purpose — the belief that quality shelter should be accessible and that real estate should serve as a tool for building lasting wealth and stronger communities.

With this vision, I founded Praise Dynasty Real Estate to become more than a property business. My goal is to build a generational company committed to making property ownership easier, delivering quality developments, and creating profitable opportunities for individuals and families.

Today, every decision we make is guided by excellence, trust, innovation, and the desire to create sustainable value for our clients and communities across Nigeria's Federal Capital Territory and beyond.
